# Welcome
Attack Vector is an online system designed for Larps to make players feel like hackers in a sci-fi world.

![Picture of several hackers cooperating hacking a site using Attack Vector](../assets/organizers/av_on_frontier.png)
*Players cooperating to solve a Netwalk ICE puzzle on Frontier 17. Photo courtesy ofÂ [Hephaestus Aperture](https://www.hephaestus-aperture.com/)*

## Overview
Players hack sites and break ICE (protection systems) by solving puzzle mini-games in order to uncover clues put there by the game masters. Emphasis is on the multiplayer aspect, players are stimulated to work as a team, not as individuals.

Players will need a laptop and a browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) to play the game. It will not work on a mobile phone.

There is a short video that showcases aspects of Attack Vector on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KOkPB3ZrHqM).

## Player
Take on the role of a hacker, no real-world hacking experience or programming experience needed. Learn how to play [here](../player/Overview).

If you want to experience Attack Vector, go to demo site: [attackvector.nl](https://attackvector.nl/). You can log in with a Google account.

## Game master
As a game master you have to create sites for your players to hack. There is a visual editor to create sites. All ICE minigames are auto-created and don't take effort on your part. More info [here](../gm/GM%20Overview).
