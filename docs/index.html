Welcome

Attack Vector is an online system designed for Larps to make players feel like hackers in a sci-fi world.

Players cooperating to solve a Netwalk ICE puzzle on Frontier 17. Photo courtesy ofÂ Hephaestus Aperture

Overviewâ€‹

Players hack sites and break ICE (protection systems) by solving puzzle mini-games in order to uncover clues put there by the game masters. Emphasis is on the multiplayer aspect, players are stimulated to work as a team, not as individuals.

Players will need a laptop and a browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) to play the game. It will not work on a mobile phone.

There is a short video that showcases aspects of Attack Vector on YouTube.

Playerâ€‹

Take on the role of a hacker, no real-world hacking experience or programming experience needed. Learn how to play here.

If you want to experience Attack Vector, go to demo site: attackvector.nl. You can log in with a Google account.

Game masterâ€‹

As a game master you have to create sites for your players to hack. There is a visual editor to create sites. All ICE minigames are auto-created and don't take effort on your part. More info here.
